How It Works

1. Upload Your Image

Select an image from your device or drag and drop it into the upload area.

2. Adjust Settings

Choose your preferred compression level, format, and target file size.

3. Compress & Download

Click the compress button and download your optimized image instantly.

Key Features

  • Batch Processing: Compress multiple images at once (coming soon)
  • Format Conversion: Convert between JPG, PNG, and WEBP formats
  • Quality Control: Fine-tune compression with precise quality settings
  • Target Size: Specify exact file size in KB or MB
  • Privacy Focused: All processing happens in your browser
  • No Watermarks: Your images remain yours

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service free to use?

Yes, our image compression tool is completely free to use with no hidden charges.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all image processing happens directly in your browser. We don't upload or store your images anywhere.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPEG, PNG, and WEBP formats for both input and output.

Is there a file size limit?

Currently, we support images up to 10MB in size. Larger files may take longer to process.
